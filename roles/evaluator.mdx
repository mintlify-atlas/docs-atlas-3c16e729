The Evaluator (role code `EVAL`) reviews assigned projects and submits rubric-based evaluations through the evaluator panel at `/evaluador/`.

## Dashboard

After logging in you are redirected to `/evaluador/`. The dashboard shows a summary of your assigned projects, pending evaluations, and any evaluations you have already submitted.

## Viewing assigned projects

Go to `/evaluador/proyectos/` to see every project the coordinator has assigned to you for the current event. Each card shows the project title, speaker name, scheduled time slot, and the current evaluation status.

## Submitting an evaluation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the evaluation form">
    From the project list at `/evaluador/proyectos/`, click **Evaluar** on the project you want to score. This opens the evaluation form at `/evaluador/formulario/<proyecto_id>/`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Score each criterion">
    The rubric criteria are listed individually. Enter a score from **1 to 5** (`valor`) for each criterion. Each criterion may include a description to guide your scoring.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add general observations">
    Use the observations field to write any overall comments about the project. This field is optional but recommended.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the evaluation">
    Click **Enviar evaluación**. The evaluation's `estado` changes from `BORRADOR` to `ENVIADA` and the record is locked.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Once you submit an evaluation (`ENVIADA`), it cannot be edited or withdrawn. Review all criterion scores and your observations carefully before clicking **Enviar evaluación**.
</Warning>

## Schedule

View your personal schedule at `/evaluador/horario/`. This page lists the time slots for all projects assigned to you in chronological order, including the assigned space.

<Note>
  SIGEP prevents the coordinator from assigning you to two projects with overlapping time slots. If you notice a scheduling conflict, contact your coordinator.
</Note>

## Evaluation history

Go to `/evaluador/historial/` to see a full record of evaluations you have submitted across all events.

## Settings

Update your profile and account preferences at `/evaluador/configuracion/`.
